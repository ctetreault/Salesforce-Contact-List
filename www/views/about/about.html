The App

This app was designed as a Proof of Concept. It connects to a Salesforce instance via OAuth, fetches all available Contacts and presents them to the user in a clean, crisp, cross-platform app for BB10, Android, and iOS.

Developed over a few weeks by Chad Tetreault, of the Enterprise Solutions team, using Cordova, Ionic, AngularJS, and Material Design concepts.
